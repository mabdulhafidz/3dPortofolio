About Me

I'm a Software Engineer. I initially chose to learn coding because I was inspired by my sibling, who motivated me. About two years ago, I started learning from scratch at school, covering topics like HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Bootstrap in the first year. I also learned design using Photoshop and some basic database concepts. In the second year, 2023, I began exploring new things at school, like PHP and Laravel. Midway through the year, there was an opportunity for a three-month internship, and I interned at PT. LSKK in Bandung. There, I learned more about the React.js framework and Tailwind CSS. As I entered the third year, I became more serious about coding. Although coding can be exhausting at times, finding solutions to bugs can be incredibly satisfying. During this third year, I delved deeper into Laravel and also explored the SvelteKit framework. In the second year, I had already learned about MongoDB, PostgreSQL, Docker, and Node.js, but those were not part of the school's curriculum, which focused more on Laravel. Despite the challenges, I've completed quite a few projects and found the experience rewarding. My strong motivation serves as a foundation to keep pushing forward because learning has no limits.
